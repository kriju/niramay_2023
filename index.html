Offerings for Good Health.

In our fast paced life of consumerism, we neglect ourselves. This accumulates over a period of time and manifests as both mental and physical forms of illnesses. At Niramay, we offer various healing techniques suitable to various needs.

Reiki

Reiki is a healing technique that promotes stress reduction and relaxation. Through gentle touch or hovering hands, practitioners believe they can channel life force energy to promote physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being.

Meditation

Meditation is a practice that involves training the mind to focus and redirect thoughts, leading to a calm and clear state of awareness. It has been found to reduce stress, improve concentration, and enhance overall mental well-being.

Hypnotherapy

Hypnotherapy is a therapeutic technique that uses guided relaxation and focused attention to achieve a heightened state of awareness. It can help individuals address various issues, such as breaking unwanted habits, reducing anxiety, and enhancing self-confidence.

Yoga for Adults

Yoga is a mind-body practice that combines physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation. It helps adults improve flexibility, build strength, relieve stress, and promote overall physical and mental well-being.

Yoga for Kids

Yoga for kids incorporates playful and age-appropriate yoga poses, breathing exercises, and relaxation techniques. It helps children develop body awareness, improve concentration, manage emotions, and cultivate a sense of inner calm and confidence.

Classes Schedule

Practice makes perfect. Attain continuous focus via our classes where we encourage self practice. Not only do we have weekly group classes, but also individual classes.
